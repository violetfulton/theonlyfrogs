Welcome

Hey I'm Froggy. Welcome to TheOnlyFrogs.

I'm really excited to show you around and enjoy snapshots of my special interests and collections. I'm a bit of a nerd and I love to collect things.
I'm a longtime fan of Animal Crossing and FFXIV. I'm also a massive collector and enjoy trying to complete my PS Vita, 3DS and PS3-5 libraries.
Although we are in the digital age I've increasingly found I want a physical copy of the things I love the most. So on the site you'll be able to see my collections of videos and music.
My favourite film is The Iron Giant. My favourite tv show includes Avatar the Last Airbender, South Park and Bones.
Music is also a big passion. My favourite bands include MCR, Tenacious D and The Smiths. I was an early fan of Ashnikko and yes I do love a singalong to a Disney movie. So come on in sit down on my lily pad and let's get croaking.
